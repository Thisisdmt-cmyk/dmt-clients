⚠ Critical Finding

No Website Means Invisible on Google

When a homeowner in Sunderland searches “flooring Whitburn”, “Karndean fitter near me”, or “carpet shop Sunderland”, they find your competitors — not you. Without a website, Google has almost nothing to index. Your showroom could be the best in the North East, but to anyone searching online, it simply doesn’t exist.

Every day without a web presence is a day that customers looking for exactly what you offer are walking into competitor showrooms instead.

⚠ Warning

Zero Google Reviews Despite 100% Facebook Recommendation

Your Facebook page tells a powerful story: 8 reviews, every single one a recommendation. 622 likes and 740 followers for a village-based independent retailer is genuinely impressive. Your customers clearly love what you do.

But Google doesn’t know that. You have 0 Google reviews, 0 Yell reviews. Meanwhile, New Image Carpets has 46 Google reviews at 4.9 stars. The gap between how good you are and how visible you are is the single biggest growth lever in your business.
