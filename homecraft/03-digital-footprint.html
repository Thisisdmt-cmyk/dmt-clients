The Roadmap

A three-phase plan to build your digital presence from the ground up. Phase 1 is free and should be done this month. Phase 2 involves modest investment over the next 3 months. Phase 3 is for when you're ready to accelerate growth.

Phase 1 — Foundation (Free)
Cost: £0 | Timeline: This Month
Get the essential free platforms set up and optimised. These two actions alone will make you visible to the majority of people searching for a roofer in your area.
  • 1
    Claim or Create Google Business Profile — Go to business.google.com and either claim your existing listing or create a new one. Add your business name (correctly spelled), phone number, service area, photos, and services. This is the number one priority. We've written the description for you in the Ready-Made Assets tab.
  • 2
    Create Facebook Business Page — Set up a proper business page (not a personal profile). See the Social Media Audit for full details. Migrate your best work photos from your personal profile.
  • 3
    Optimise Bark Profile — Update your Bark listing with the correct business name, full service list, and your best before-and-after photos. Ask recent customers for Bark reviews.
  • 4
    Start Collecting Reviews — After every job, send a WhatsApp message asking for a Google review. Use the templates from the Social Media Audit. Aim for 10 reviews in the first month.
Phase 2 — Expansion (Moderate Cost)
Cost: £100 – £500 | Timeline: Months 2–4
Expand your presence across trade directories and get a simple website up. This increases the number of places customers can find you and adds credibility.
  • 5
    Register on Trade Directories — Sign up for Checkatrade, MyBuilder, and Rated People. Some have free tiers; others charge a monthly fee. Start with one and expand. See the suggested list in Ready-Made Assets.
  • 6
    Create a Simple Website — A one-page website with your services, service area, photos, phone number, and a contact form. Options range from free (Google Sites, Carrd) to affordable (£100–300 for a basic WordPress site). It doesn't need to be fancy — it needs to exist.
  • 7
    List on Free Directories — Yell, FreeIndex, and Thomson Local all offer free basic listings. These help with Google search visibility even if they don't generate many direct leads.
Phase 3 — Growth (Investment)
Cost: £100+/Month | Timeline: Month 6+
Once your foundation is solid and you have reviews coming in, consider paid advertising and video content to accelerate growth.
  • 8
    Google Ads (Local Service Ads) — Google's "Local Service Ads" appear at the very top of search results with a "Google Guaranteed" badge. You only pay when someone contacts you. Extremely effective for trades but requires a verified Google Business Profile first.
  • 9
    Video Content — Short before-and-after video clips of jobs perform incredibly well on Facebook and Instagram. You don't need professional equipment — a phone on a tripod and a 30-second walkthrough is enough. Time-lapse videos of roof work are particularly engaging.
  • 10
    Facebook Ads (Targeted Local) — Once your business page is established with reviews and content, run targeted ads to homeowners within your service area. Even £5/day can generate significant local visibility.
