About

WHAT IS THE PILOT?

A genuine partnership where we both gain something valuable.

DMT Consulting is a new digital consultancy built to help small businesses and independent artists grow their presence online. We know the work is good — but every consultancy needs a track record to point to.

That's where the Pilot Programme comes in. We're offering a limited number of engagements at no charge to build our portfolio and prove the model. You receive the exact same quality of work we deliver to commercial clients — assessments worth £1,500 – £1,650 — and in return, you give us an honest testimonial and permission to use your project as a case study.

This isn't charity, and it isn't a sales funnel. It's a straightforward exchange: we do real work for you, you help us build credibility. We both walk away better off.

In Return

WHAT WE ASK

We believe in being upfront. Here's exactly what we need from you.

  • An honest testimonial we can use on our website and marketing materials — this can be anonymous if you prefer
  • Permission to use your project as a case study — your business or artist name, our findings, and the deliverables we produced
  • Willingness to provide feedback on our process — what worked, what didn't, what we could do better
  • Completion of a brief satisfaction survey after we deliver your work — takes five minutes
That's it. No hidden catches. No upsell. No ongoing commitment. If you're happy with the work, say so. If you're not, say that too — we want honest feedback, not flattery.

  • Priority given to businesses and artists who would genuinely benefit most — we're looking for people where our work can have real impact
