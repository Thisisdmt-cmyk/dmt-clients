Phase 1
Quick Wins — This Month, £0 Cost

Six opportunities you can action this week with nothing more than your phone and a bit of time.

Google Review Generation System

01

Send every customer a WhatsApp message after their job is complete asking for a Google review. Include a direct link. Target: 5+ reviews per week to reach 100+ within 6 months.

High Impact Low Effort £0
  1. Create your direct Google review link (search "Google review link generator")
  2. Save the review request messages from the Ready-Made Assets tab as WhatsApp templates
  3. Send within 2 hours of job completion while satisfaction is highest
  4. Send a follow-up after 3 days if they haven't reviewed
  5. Reply to every review (positive and negative) within 24 hours

MOT Reminder Text Messages

02

Collect every customer's MOT due date and phone number. Send a text 4 weeks before, 1 week before, and the day after if overdue. This alone could recover 30-40% of lost repeat business.

High Impact Low Effort £0
  1. Start a simple spreadsheet: Customer Name, Phone, Reg, MOT Due Date
  2. Log every vehicle that comes through the door from today
  3. Check MOT due dates via the DVSA tool for existing customers
  4. Set weekly calendar reminders to check who's due in the next 4 weeks
  5. Send the reminder templates from the Ready-Made Assets tab

Upsell on Every Job

03

When a car is on the ramp for any job, do a quick visual inspection. If you spot anything, use the upsell conversation script. Even a 20% conversion on advisory items adds up fast.

High Impact Low Effort £0
  1. Make the visual check a standard part of every job (tyres, brakes, fluid levels, lights)
  2. Use the upsell script from the Ready-Made Assets tab — it's non-pushy and helpful
  3. Always give a price while the car is there ("saves on labour since we've got access")
  4. If they decline now, text them a reminder in 4-6 weeks
  5. Track conversion: how many advisory items become booked work?

Optimise Your Google Business Profile

04

Your Google listing is your shopfront. Add photos weekly, post updates, list every service you offer, and make sure your hours, phone number, and address are spot on.

Medium Impact Low Effort £0
  1. Log in to Google Business Profile and complete every field
  2. Add 5-10 photos: workshop, team, recovery vehicles, completed jobs (with permission)
  3. Add all services: MOT, servicing, diagnostics, tyres, recovery, brakes, exhausts, etc.
  4. Post a Google update once a week (special offer, tip, completed job photo)
  5. Enable messaging so customers can reach you directly from Google

WhatsApp Business for Bookings

05

Set up WhatsApp Business (free) with a catalogue of services, automated greeting message, and quick replies. Many customers prefer texting over calling, and it's easier to manage than voicemail.

Medium Impact Low Effort £0
  1. Download WhatsApp Business (free) and register your number 07487 738527
  2. Set up your business profile with address, hours, and description
  3. Create a catalogue listing your core services with prices
  4. Set an auto-greeting: "Thanks for messaging Tyne & Wear Garage! We'll get back to you shortly."
  5. Create quick replies for common questions (MOT price, opening hours, booking)

Post-Service Follow-Up

06

A quick "how's the car running?" text 48 hours after a repair shows you care, catches any issues early, and is the perfect moment to ask for a referral or review.

Medium Impact Low Effort £0
  1. Set a reminder for 48 hours after every completed job
  2. Send a short WhatsApp: "Hi [Name], just checking in — how's the car running after the [work done]?"
  3. If they're happy, send the review request link
  4. If there's an issue, get them straight back in (builds massive trust)
  5. Ask if they know anyone else who might need work done
Phase 2
Mid-Term Wins — 1-3 Months, £0-500

Five bigger moves that need a little more setup but deliver serious returns.

Launch the Tyne & Wear Care Plan

07

A monthly subscription: MOT + annual service + 10% off all repairs for £25/month. Creates predictable recurring revenue and locks customers in. 40 members = £12,000/year guaranteed. See the ready-made plan card in the Assets tab.

High Impact Medium Effort £0
  1. Print the Care Plan card from the Ready-Made Assets tab
  2. Offer it to every customer at the point of invoice: "Have you heard about our Care Plan?"
  3. Set up a simple standing order or use a payment link (Stripe, GoCardless)
  4. Track members in your spreadsheet: Name, Start Date, MOT Due, Service Due
  5. Target 10 sign-ups in the first month from existing loyal customers

Fleet & Trade Accounts

08

Target local businesses with 3+ vehicles: taxi firms, delivery companies, tradespeople, letting agents. Offer priority booking and fixed pricing. One fleet of 10 vehicles could be worth £5,000-15,000/year.

High Impact Medium Effort £0-100
  1. List 20 local businesses with fleets (Google Maps search: taxi, courier, plumber, electrician)
  2. Personalise and send the fleet pitch letter from the Ready-Made Assets tab
  3. Offer a free vehicle health check as an intro (no obligation)
  4. Create a simple fleet pricing sheet: MOT, service, tyres, recovery at trade rates
  5. Follow up by phone within 5 days of sending the letter

Seasonal Campaigns

09

Run a targeted campaign each month based on seasonal needs: winter checks in October, AC recharge in spring, pre-holiday vehicle checks in May. Creates urgency and gives you a reason to contact customers.

Medium Impact Medium Effort £0-200
  1. Use the seasonal calendar from the Ready-Made Assets tab
  2. Create a simple WhatsApp broadcast list of your customers
  3. Send one message per month with the seasonal offer
  4. Post the same offer on Google Business Profile
  5. Track which campaigns generate the most bookings

Recovery Vehicle Branding

10

Your recovery trucks are mobile billboards driving across Tyne & Wear every day. Professional livery with your phone number, services, and a clear call-to-action turns every breakdown job into free advertising.

Medium Impact Medium Effort £300-500
  1. Get 2-3 quotes from local vehicle graphics companies
  2. Include: business name, phone number, "MOT | Servicing | Repairs | Recovery", website
  3. Use large, bold text readable at distance — phone number is the priority
  4. Add "24/7 Recovery" prominently if applicable
  5. Consider a QR code linking to your Google Business Profile

Referral Incentive Programme

11

Offer existing customers £20 off their next service for every new customer they refer who books work. Word-of-mouth is already your biggest channel — this turbocharges it.

Medium Impact Low Effort £20/referral
  1. Create simple referral cards (business card size): "Refer a friend, get £20 off"
  2. Hand one to every customer with their invoice
  3. Track referrals: who referred whom, and credit their account
  4. Mention it in your post-service follow-up text
  5. The new customer gets 10% off their first job too (everyone wins)
