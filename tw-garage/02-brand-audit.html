Brand Fragmentation Is the Core Issue

Your business is trading under three different names across platforms. Customers searching for you online are finding inconsistent information — or nothing at all. The good news: your Google reviews are strong and the fixes are straightforward.

N

Name Consistency

Three different names in use: "Tyne and Wear Garage & tyre service's" on Facebook, a different variation on Google, and the real trading name elsewhere. This dilutes search visibility and confuses customers.

2/10
V

Visual Identity

No formal logo, brand guidelines, or consistent visual system. Without a recognisable mark, every touchpoint is a missed branding opportunity.

4/10

Shopfront

5/10

2/10
