THE PLAN

Five steps, roughly 90 minutes of total work, and your Google presence goes from a 3/10 to a potential 9/10. Step 1 is urgent — do it today. The rest can follow over the next week.

1

CLAIM YOUR PROFILE

⏰ 15 minutes • URGENT — do this today
▼

This is the single most important action in this entire document. Everything else depends on it. Until you claim the profile, you have zero control over how your business appears to thousands of people searching Google each month.

1. Go to Google Business Profile Manager:

https://business.google.com

Sign in with a Google account. If you don't have one, create one using your business email or phone number. Use an account you will always have access to — not a personal account you might lose.

2. Search for your business:
Type "Tyne & Wear Garage and Recovery" in the search box. Your existing listing should appear with the address at 6 Laygate. Select it.

3. Click "Claim this business":
You will see a prompt indicating this business has not been claimed. Click the claim button and confirm you are authorised to manage this business.

4. Verify ownership via phone:
The fastest method is phone verification. Google will call or text 07487 738527 with a verification code. Enter the code and you are verified instantly.

If phone is not offered, you can verify by postcard (5–14 days) or video verification. Phone is strongly recommended.

5. Done.
Once verified, you now control your listing. All 17 existing reviews stay attached to your profile. You can now update every detail, respond to reviews, add photos, and post updates.

3

UPLOAD PHOTOS

⏰ 20 minutes • Do within the first week
▼

Listings with photos receive significantly more engagement. In your Google Business dashboard, go to Photos and upload the following (use the checklist in the Ready-Made Assets tab):

Minimum to upload:

• Shopfront/exterior (well-lit, daytime)
• Workshop interior showing your equipment
• Team photo (builds trust and shows you are real people)
• Your recovery vehicle (key selling point)
• Examples of completed work
• MOT bay

Photo tips: Use landscape orientation, good lighting, and make sure images are clear and professional. Phone photos are fine — just ensure they are not blurry or dark. Aim for at least 5–10 photos to start, then add more monthly.

4

START A REVIEW CAMPAIGN

⏰ Ongoing • Start this week
▼

Your goal: 2–3 new reviews per week. At this rate, you will surpass North East Vehicle Repairs within 3 months.

How to get your review link:

1. Go to your Google Business dashboard
2. Click "Ask for reviews" (or find it under Home > Get more reviews)
3. Copy the short link provided — this is your unique review URL

When to ask: Send the review request within 1–2 hours of the customer collecting their vehicle. The experience is still fresh. Do not wait until the next day.

How to ask: Use the ready-made WhatsApp and text message templates in the Ready-Made Assets tab. Simply paste the message and swap in the customer's name and your review link.

Respond to every review — positive and negative. Templates for responses are also in the Ready-Made Assets tab. Responding signals to Google that you are an active, engaged business.

5

POST WEEKLY GOOGLE POSTS

⏰ 5 minutes each • Once per week
▼

Google Posts appear directly on your listing and signal to Google that your business is active. They expire after 7 days, so posting weekly keeps your profile fresh.

How to post:

1. Go to your Google Business dashboard
2. Click "Add update" (or Posts section)
3. Write a short post (150–300 words) with a photo
4. Add a call-to-action button (e.g., "Call now", "Book online")

What to post: Rotate between offers, seasonal tips, service spotlights, and community thank-you posts. Pre-written templates for all of these are in the Ready-Made Assets tab.

Pro tip: Take a photo of each completed job (with the customer's permission) and use that as the post image. Real photos always outperform stock images.
