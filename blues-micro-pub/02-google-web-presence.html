📈 Head-to-Head: Blues vs. National Champion

Tamworth Tap Comparison

Tamworth Tap is the three-time CAMRA National Pub of the Year (2022, 2023, 2025). Blue's holds the Branch-level award — but competes surprisingly well on digital metrics.

Quick summary: Blues wins on Google rating (4.9 vs 4.8) and Instagram (2,200 followers vs 1,792; 1,745 posts vs 33). Tamworth leads on review volume (619 Google reviews vs 119; 91 TripAdvisor reviews vs 8) and has a custom website.

⚡ 6-Step Action Plan

Designed for two people. No step requires a budget. Priority order matters — start at the top.

1

Claim Your Untappd Venue

Visit untappd.com/venue/claim and search for "Blue's Micro Pub". Verify ownership with your business details. Once claimed, you can update your live beer menu, respond to check-ins, and appear in "nearby venues" for every Untappd user visiting the coast.

Priority 1 5 minutes Free
2

Grow TripAdvisor Reviews to 25+

With only 8 reviews, every new one moves the needle. Place the TripAdvisor review card (see Assets tab) by the till. Mention TripAdvisor when tourists or coast walkers visit — they're the most likely to leave reviews. A target of 25 reviews over the next 3 months would transform your ranking.

  • Focus on visitors and tourists (locals tend to use Google)
  • Never offer incentives for reviews — just ask genuinely
  • Respond to every review, even short ones
Priority 2 Ongoing Free
3

Start Google Posts (1 per Week)

Google Posts appear directly in your Business Profile when people search for you. They boost your SEO, show Google you're an active business, and give searchers a reason to visit. We've pre-written 6 weeks of posts in the Assets tab — just copy, paste, and publish.

  • Log into Google Business Profile, click "Add update"
  • Paste the post text, add a photo, select the CTA button
  • Posts expire after 7 days, so weekly is the right rhythm
Priority 3 10 min/week Free
4

Update Google Business Description

Your Google Business Profile description is the first thing many people read about Blue's. A well-written, keyword-rich description helps you appear in more searches. We've written one for you in the Assets tab — just copy and paste it into your profile settings.

Priority 4 5 minutes Free
5

Update TripAdvisor Description

Same principle as Google. A strong TripAdvisor description helps with discoverability, especially for tourists planning coastal walks or day trips. Copy-paste version is in the Assets tab.

Priority 5 5 minutes Free
6

Keep Growing Google Reviews

119 reviews at 4.9 stars is outstanding, but growing toward 200+ reviews would make Blue's dominant in local search. The "Leave us a Google Review" card (Assets tab) does the heavy lifting — place it by the till and let it work passively.

  • Respond to every Google review (positive and negative)
  • Aim for 2–3 new reviews per week
  • Never buy reviews or offer incentives — they get flagged and removed
Priority 6 Ongoing Free
