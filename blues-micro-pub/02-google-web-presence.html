📄 Suggested Website Structure

▼

If you upgrade or rebuild the site, here's the minimum page structure with content guidance:

Home

Hero image with CAMRA Pub of the Year 2026 badge. Tagline. Quick snapshot of what's on tap today. Next upcoming event. Opening hours. Link to Google Maps.

About Us

Cal and Alice's story — how Blue's was born in 2020, the journey to CAMRA Pub of the Year. The micropub philosophy: independent beer, community spirit, friendly welcome. Photos of the team.

What's On

Events calendar: Cask Club (Wed/Thu/Sun), Pie & Pint Thursday, Running Club Thursday, Whisky Club (1st Monday). Seasonal events and one-offs. Each event with description, time, and what to expect.

Beer Board

Current taps — updated regularly. 5 cask ales, 6 real ciders, 9 keg lines. Brewery names, beer styles, ABV. Could integrate with Untappd. A reason for people to check your site before visiting.

Find Us

Embedded Google Map. Full address. Opening hours (with Whisky Club exception). "Card payments only" notice. Parking tips. Public transport options. Nearby walking routes.

Awards

CAMRA Pub of the Year 2026. CAMRA Cider Pub of the Year 2026. Any regional wins. Press coverage links (Shields Gazette, Sunderland Echo). Photos from award ceremonies.
