Your 6-Step Action Plan

Realistic steps for a two-person team. Each one tells you what to do, how long it takes, and what the payoff is.

1

Facebook Health Check This Week

Check your Facebook page is set to Public so customers can find you. Complete every field: address, opening hours, phone number, website link. Update the cover photo with something that shows off the pub. Add your CAMRA Branch Pub of the Year 2026 and Branch Cider Pub of the Year 2026 wins to the bio, About section, and a pinned post.

⏱ 30 minutes 🎯 Unlocks visibility to every local searching for you
2

Consistent Facebook Posting Start This Week

Post 3–4 times per week on Facebook. Your content pillars: beer board updates (what's on tap today), event announcements (Whisky Club, Pie & Pint, Running Club, Cheeseboard Sunday), behind-the-scenes with Cal & Alice, and the CAMRA wins. Cross-post your best Instagram content — don't reinvent the wheel. Use the weekly planner in Tab 3.

⏱ 15-20 min/week 🎯 Active page = trust signal for new customers
3

Sharpen Your Instagram This Month

You're already strong here with 2,200 followers and 1,745 posts. Keep posting 2–3 times per week on the feed. The next level: start creating short Reels (15–30 seconds). A cask pour, the cider lineup, the pub filling up on a Thursday night. Reels are how Instagram pushes your content to people who don't follow you yet. One good Reel can reach 10x your follower count. Add the CAMRA wins to your bio if they're not there already.

⏱ 20 min/week (what you're already doing) 🎯 Reels unlock new audiences beyond your 2,200 followers
4

Google Reviews Push This Month

You have 4.9 stars — outstanding. The goal now is volume. Print the "Review Us" tent card from Tab 3 (add your Google review QR code — generate one free at qr-code-generator.com) and put it on the bar. Mention it at the end of events. Every review improves your ranking in local search results. Target: 200+ reviews within 6 months. The tent card takes 2 minutes to set up and works 24/7.

⏱ 10 min to print & place tent card 🎯 More reviews = higher Google ranking = more walk-ins
5

Claim Untappd & Grow TripAdvisor This Month

Verify your Untappd venue listing at untappd.com — it takes a few minutes and lets beer tourists find you. For TripAdvisor, encourage visitors to leave reviews (especially tourists and CAMRA members who travel for pubs). You have 8 reviews vs Tamworth Tap's 91 — this is a quick win waiting to happen.

⏱ 15 min to claim Untappd; ongoing for reviews 🎯 Beer tourists use these platforms to plan visits
6

TikTok (When You're Ready) Next Quarter

Only when steps 1–5 are humming. Set up @bluesmicropub on TikTok. Post 1 video per week — cross-post every TikTok to Instagram Reels. Your first video idea: "We just won CAMRA Branch Pub of the Year. Here's our little micropub in Whitburn." Walk through the pub. Show the ales. Keep it authentic. You don't need fancy editing.

⏱ 20 min/week for 1 video 🎯 Reaches younger audiences who don't use Facebook
