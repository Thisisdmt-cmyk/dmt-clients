CAMRA Pub of the Year 2025

Content & Events Strategy

A 12-month plan for what to post, where to post it, and how to fill Blue's Micro Pub every night of the week.

The Five Content Pillars

Every post you publish should fit into one of these five themes. Rotate them to keep the feed varied, strategic, and never repetitive.

01

The Pour

Beer & Cider Content

What's on tap, tasting notes, cider of the week, supplier stories. This is the bread and butter of pub content — people want to know what they'll be drinking before they arrive.

3x weekly
02

The Crown

CAMRA Pub of the Year

Award celebrations, press coverage shares, behind-the-scenes of what it means to be Britain's best pub. This is your biggest differentiator — use it relentlessly.

2x weekly (first month) then weekly
03

The Crew

People Content

Cal & Alice, the regulars (with permission), staff stories, community moments. People connect with people, not brands. Show the faces behind the bar.

2x weekly
04

The Calendar

Events Content

Upcoming events, event recaps, countdowns, live coverage. Every event is three pieces of content: the announcement, the event itself, and the recap.

Daily during events, 3x weekly otherwise
05

The Village

Whitburn & Local

Village life, coastal walks ending at the pub, local partnerships, community events. Position Blue's as the heart of Whitburn, not just a place to drink.

Platform Strategy

Facebook Primary

This is where pub customers are. The engine room.

Post5–7x per week
FormatPhotos + short copy. Events as Facebook Events.
Best times11am–1pm (lunch browsers), 4–6pm (after-work crowd)
Key action: Create a Facebook Event for EVERY recurring night — Cask Club, Pie & Pint, Whisky Club, Running Club. This is the single biggest quick win.

Instagram Secondary

Reach the younger craft beer audience. Visual-first.

Feed3–4x per week (quality photos, carousels)
StoriesDaily — beer board, atmosphere, behind scenes
Reels2x weekly (pouring shots, pub atmosphere, staff picks)
Best times12pm, 5pm, 8pm
Key action: Use Stories for real-time beer board updates. Photograph the board daily and post it — this alone drives visits.

TikTok Growth

The biggest untapped opportunity for pubs right now.

Post3x weekly minimum
FormatShort 15–30s clips. Cask ale pulls, cider pours, pub atmosphere.
Content"POV: You're at Britain's best pub", customer reactions, behind the bar
Key action: Film 5 videos in one session, schedule across the week. Trending audio + pub content = massive reach potential.

Google Posts SEO

Appears directly in Google search results. Massively underused by pubs.

Post2x weekly
ContentEvents, beer highlights, award news, seasonal offers
Key action: Every time someone Googles "Blue's Micro Pub" or "pubs near Whitburn", your latest post appears. Two posts a week, massive return.

Newsletter Email

"The Blues Brief" — direct line to your most loyal fans.

FrequencyMonthly (first of each month)
SectionsWhat's On, Beer of the Month, Event Recap, Meet the Brewer, Subscriber Exclusive
PlatformMailchimp (free up to 500 subscribers)
Key action: Email signup card at the bar, QR code on tables. Build the list from day one — this audience is yours forever, no algorithm.
