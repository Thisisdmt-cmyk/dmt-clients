Content & Events Strategy

A 12-month plan built around what already works — extending your Instagram strength across platforms and filling every night of the week.

Your Instagram Is Already Exceptional

With 2,200 followers and 1,745 posts, you outperform the three-time CAMRA National Pub of the Year on Instagram by 23% on followers and 53x on content volume. For a two-person micropub, that is a genuine competitive advantage. This strategy does not ask you to start from scratch — it builds on what you have already proven you can do, and extends it to platforms where your customers are also looking for you.

Data Sources — Content pillars were derived from analysis of your actual Instagram posting history (1,745 posts). Recurring events (Pie & Pint Thursday, Running Club, Cheeseboard Sunday, Whisky Club) are sourced from your social media and Google listing. Seasonal events in the 12-month calendar are suggestions based on UK hospitality patterns and should be confirmed against your actual plans. Posting frequencies are calibrated for two people running a pub — not a marketing agency.

Five Content Pillars

You already post instinctively under these themes. We identified these from your actual Instagram feed — naming them makes it easier to stay balanced and never run out of ideas. Each pillar has a realistic frequency for two people running a pub.

1x per week (more in the first month)

Monthly Specials

One special event per month, on top of your regulars. Each one is designed to generate content, attract new visitors, and give your community something to look forward to.

April 2026

Spring Launch
Monthly Special
Easter Weekend Ale Trail
Curated selection of spring ales across the Easter weekend. Tasting cards for customers to rate each one. Social push around "Easter at your Branch Pub of the Year." Photo opportunity at every pour.
Plus your regulars: Whisky Club (Mon 6th), Pie & Pint & Running Club (every Thu), Free Cheeseboard (every Sun)
Local events: Pier to Pier Race (Sun 19th, ~1,000 runners pass through Whitburn — post-race trade opportunity). South Shields parkrun every Saturday along the coast.

May 2026

Bank Holiday Season
Monthly Special
Cider Month Showcase
May is traditionally cider month. Expand your already strong cider range with guest ciders. A daily cider spotlight on Stories, and a cider flight option at the bar. You already won CAMRA Branch Cider Pub of the Year — own this month.
Plus your regulars: Whisky Club (Mon 4th), Pie & Pint & Running Club (every Thu), Free Cheeseboard (every Sun). Bank Holiday weekends for extra footfall.
Local events: Great North Feast at Bents Park (Sat 23–Mon 25 May, free food & drink festival). Sunderland City Runs (16–17 May, up to 4,000 runners). SAFC Premier League home fixtures.

June 2026

Summer Begins
Monthly Special
Meet the Brewer Evening
Invite a North East brewery (one you already stock) to present their beers. Free entry, casual format, Q&A over pints. Film everything for content — one evening generates a week of posts. Cross-promote with the brewery's social channels.
Plus your regulars: Whisky Club (Mon 1st), Pie & Pint & Running Club (every Thu), Free Cheeseboard (every Sun). Father's Day (21st) — push gift cards.
Local events: South Tyneside Festival programme begins. Whitburn Cricket Club home fixtures (walking distance). 70,000 annual visitors to Souter/Coastal Park — peak season starts.

July 2026

Peak Summer
Monthly Special
Summer Session Saturday
One Saturday with an extended tap range and live acoustic music. Keep it simple — a local musician, your best summer ales, and a good atmosphere. If it works well, repeat in August. Content goldmine for Reels and Stories.
Plus your regulars: Whisky Club (Mon 6th), Pie & Pint & Running Club (every Thu), Free Cheeseboard (every Sun)
Local events: South Tyneside Sunday Concerts at Bents Park (free, up to 20,000 per week). Mouth of the Tyne Festival (9–12 Jul, Tynemouth). Peak tourist season — Souter/Coastal Park at highest footfall.

August 2026

Summer Sessions
Monthly Special
Beer & Cheese Pairing Night
Ticketed evening, limited to 20 people. Five beers paired with five cheeses. Premium experience at a micropub scale. Tickets sell themselves on social. Builds on what you already do with Cheeseboard Sunday — this is the elevated version.
Plus your regulars: Whisky Club (Mon 3rd), Pie & Pint & Running Club (every Thu), Free Cheeseboard (every Sun). Bank Holiday weekend for extra footfall.
Local events: Great North Feast at Bents Park (Sat 29–Mon 31 Aug, free food & drink festival). South Tyneside Sunday Concerts continue. New SAFC Premier League season typically starts mid-August.

September 2026

Cask Ale Week
Monthly Special
Cask Ale Week Celebration
CAMRA's national Cask Ale Week. As Branch Pub of the Year, you should own this in your area. Extended cask range for the week, daily tap features on social, and a "best of cask" night on the Saturday. Contact CAMRA branch for cross-promotion.
Plus your regulars: Whisky Club (Mon 7th), Pie & Pint & Running Club (every Thu), Free Cheeseboard (every Sun)
Local events: Great North Run (Sun 13th) — 60,000 runners, final mile along the coast near Whitburn/Marsden. The biggest footfall event of the year. Plan extended hours. CAMRA SST Beer & Cider Festival (mid-Sep, East Boldon). Cricket season ends.

October 2026

Dark Beer Season
Monthly Special
Halloween Ales Night
Dark ales, porters, stouts. Themed atmosphere for one evening — nothing elaborate, just the right beers and maybe some candles. Great for moody photography and Reels content. Tie it to the seasonal tap board transition into darker beers.
Plus your regulars: Whisky Club (Mon 5th), Pie & Pint & Running Club (every Thu), Free Cheeseboard (every Sun)

November 2026

Fireside Season
Monthly Special
Bonfire Night Warmer
Position Blue's as the pre or post-fireworks pub. Warming ales, maybe a mulled cider. If there are local firework displays in Whitburn or Sunderland, time it right. One post linking to the local display + your opening hours does the work.
Plus your regulars: Whisky Club (Mon 2nd), Pie & Pint & Running Club (every Thu), Free Cheeseboard (every Sun).
Local events: Remembrance Sunday (8th) — Whitburn parade assembles at Parish Church 10:40am, service at War Memorial. Respectful acknowledgement; post-service trade. Christmas lights switch-on (late Nov, South Shields).

December 2026

Festive Season
Monthly Special
12 Ales of Christmas
A different guest ale each day from 13th–24th December. Daily social content writes itself — photograph the pump clip, post tasting notes, let customers vote for their favourite. Advent-style countdowns perform brilliantly on social media. Push gift cards hard throughout December.
Plus your regulars: Whisky Club (Mon 7th), Pie & Pint & Running Club (every Thu), Free Cheeseboard (every Sun). Small Business Saturday (5th) — cross-promote with Whitburn independents. NYE — keep it simple, no need for a ticketed event unless demand is clear.

January 2027

New Year
Monthly Special
Burns Night Whisky Special (25th)
A natural fit for your Whisky Club community. Scottish whiskies, maybe a haggis pie for Pie & Pint that week. Your Whisky Club regulars will bring friends. One event, two audiences (whisky fans + Burns Night crowd).
Plus your regulars: Whisky Club (Mon 5th — or move to 25th for Burns Night), Pie & Pint & Running Club (every Thu), Free Cheeseboard (every Sun). "Damp January" messaging — promote low-ABV and AF options without being preachy.

February 2027

Anniversary Month
Monthly Special
7th Anniversary Celebration
Blue's opened 5th February 2020. Seven years. Throwback content all week, a special anniversary ale if you can arrange one with a local brewery, and a thank-you post to the regulars who have been there since day one. Genuine, heartfelt, no gimmicks.
Plus your regulars: Whisky Club (Mon 2nd), Pie & Pint & Running Club (every Thu), Free Cheeseboard (every Sun). Valentine's — no need for a special event, but a simple "date night at the pub" post works.

March 2027

Year One Complete
Monthly Special
St Patrick's Day Session (17th)
Irish whiskeys for Whisky Club, Irish-style stouts on tap, and a reason for midweek footfall. Simple theming, good craic, strong social content. Tie in with the anniversary of the CAMRA Branch win — celebrate a full year holding the title.
Plus your regulars: Whisky Club (Mon 3rd), Pie & Pint & Running Club (every Thu), Free Cheeseboard (every Sun). Mother's Day — push gift cards. Spring ales start returning to the board.
